Unfinished Business

Launch of the new LG refrigerator model TheOrgVenFridge

  • Soda dispenser feature

    The refrigerator will have a soda dispenser feature alongside existing water dispenser that displays a variety of beverages on the screen for the consumer to choose from. There will also be a feature for the exactly desired amount of ice.

  • Vending machine feature

    TheOrgVenFridge will feature the vending machine feature which dispenses out the desired item out of the fridge with the click of a button on the display screen or the ThinQ app. This conserves time and also energy/electricity that is usually wasted looking around while having the fridge door open.

  • Automatic sorter

    This refrigerator also features an automatic sorter which automatically sorts the food in any arrangement the consumer pleases all done using the ThinQ app.
